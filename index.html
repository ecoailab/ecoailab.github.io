Latest News

Recent updates and achievements from our lab

March 2026

New Project on Quantum Battery Ultra-Fast Charging

EcoAI Lab has secured a new research grant from the Korea Institute of Energy Technology to develop ultra-fast charging technology for quantum batteries.

January 2025

LLM-based Conversational AI Project Launched

We are partnering with the Korea Institute of Energy Research to develop an LLM-based conversational AI for energy resource health diagnosis.

September 2024

EcoAI Lab Officially Established

Prof. Sangkeum Lee has officially launched the EcoAI Lab at Hanbat National University, focusing on AI for environmental and energy optimization.

Research Areas

Our core domains of investigation and innovation

Sensor Network Optimization

Applying deep learning to optimize sensor networks for achieving carbon zero goals and environmental sustainability.

Smart Factory & Home Energy

Developing intelligent systems for energy management and optimization in industrial and residential environments.

IoT and Security Sensors

Enhancing the security and reliability of Internet of Things devices and sensor networks through advanced AI techniques.

Quantum Computing & Battery

Exploring the intersection of quantum computing and energy storage, focusing on quantum battery technologies.

Reinforcement Learning & Agents

Designing autonomous AI agents using reinforcement learning for complex decision-making and optimization tasks.

Big Data & Energy Optimization

Analyzing large-scale datasets to uncover patterns and optimize energy consumption across various sectors.

Our Team

The minds behind the innovation

Sangkeum Lee (이상금)

Assistant Professor, Lab Director

Prof. Sangkeum Lee leads the EcoAI Lab at Hanbat National University. His research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and environmental energy optimization.

  • 2023 - Present: Assistant Professor, Hanbat National University
  • 2021 - 2023: Senior Researcher, ETRI
  • 2020 - 2021: Post-Doc, KAIST
  • 2018 - 2020: Ph.D., KAIST CCS Graduate School
  • 2016 - 2018: M.S., KAIST
  • 2009 - 2016: B.S., Korea University
