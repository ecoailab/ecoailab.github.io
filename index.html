Research Areas

What We Study

Six core domains at the intersection of artificial intelligence and environmental energy.

Sensor Network Optimization
Applying deep learning to optimize sensor networks for achieving carbon zero goals.
Smart Factory & Home Energy
Intelligent AI systems for energy management and optimization in industrial and residential environments.
IoT and Security Sensors
Enhancing security and reliability of IoT devices through advanced AI techniques.
Quantum Computing & Battery
Exploring quantum computing and energy storage, focusing on quantum battery technologies.
Reinforcement Learning & Agents
Designing autonomous AI agents using reinforcement learning for complex decision-making.
Big Data & Energy Optimization
Analyzing large-scale datasets to uncover patterns and optimize energy consumption.
